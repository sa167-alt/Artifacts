## Practical Information for Delegates

**Simultaneous Interpretation**
Available in the Auditorium (Hall 2) in Spanish (ES), French (FR), and German (DE). English is the default session language.

**Dietary Requirements**
- Main event allergen meals must be pre-registered by **25 August 2026**.
- Gala Dinner dietary pre-registration closes earlier: **20 August 2026**.
- Kosher meals require 10-day advance notice — please register at booking.

**Workshop Registration**
The AI Safety Frameworks Workshop (Day 1, 16:00) is capped at 60 participants. Pre-registration is required — secure your place in the Summit app.

**Accessibility**
- All event spaces are step-free accessible.
- Hearing loops are installed in the Auditorium and Room A1.
- Portable hearing systems available for Rooms B2, C1, and Workshop Hall W1 — request in advance via the accessibility desk.
- Accessible parking: 42 spaces on P2 level — pass issued on registration.
- Guide dog relief area: Main Lobby North exit.
- Delegates requiring lift assistance to the Rooftop Terrace should notify the registration desk on arrival.

**Badge & Access**
Your NFC badge grants access to all general sessions. Invite-only sessions (Investor Roundtable, VIP areas) require a separate access tier — check your invitation.

**No Food or Drink in the Auditorium**
Catering is provided in the Hall 3 Foyer, Hall 4 (Dining), and Hall 5 Corridor only.

**Summit App**
Download before arrival. The app carries the live schedule, Q&A voting, Workshop materials (released 48 hrs prior), and Demo audience voting.
