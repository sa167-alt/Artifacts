# Barcelona AI Summit 2026 — Attendee Schedule

**Dates:** 14-16 September 2026
**Venue:** Fira Barcelona Gran Via, L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, Barcelona
**Theme:** *Intelligence with Integrity: AI Governance, Innovation & Human Impact*

> All main event spaces are step-free accessible. Hearing loop available in Auditorium and Room A1.
