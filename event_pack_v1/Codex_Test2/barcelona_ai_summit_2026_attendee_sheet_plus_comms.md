### Accessibility and venue guidance for attendees

- All main event spaces are step-free accessible.
- Hearing loops are installed in the Auditorium and Room A1 only.
- Dedicated accessible seating is available in the Auditorium and breakout rooms.
- Accessible parking is available in P2 and passes are issued on registration.
- Guide dog relief area is located at the Main Lobby North exit.
- If the Welcome Reception is held on the Rooftop Terrace, lift capacity is limited and shared with catering, so delegates needing lift access should notify the team in advance.

Source: `venue_constraints.txt` lines 102-112.

### Food, drink, and session-room guidance

- No food or beverages are permitted in the Auditorium.
- Alcohol is served only at the reception and Gala Dinner, not in session rooms.
- All catering is managed through the venue's in-house caterer.

Source: `venue_constraints.txt` lines 42-44 and 119-126.
