# Barcelona AI Summit 2026 — Attendee Sheet

Current as of 2026-04-07. Built from the event programme, venue constraints register, operator brief, and calendar export in this workspace.

## Event Basics

- Dates: 14-16 September 2026
- Venue: Fira Barcelona Gran Via, Av. Joan Carles I, 64, L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, 08908 Barcelona
- Theme: Intelligence with Integrity: AI Governance, Innovation & Human Impact
- Main event spaces are step-free accessible.

## Important Attendee Notes

- Registration opens at 08:00 on Monday, 14 September in the Main Lobby, Hall 1 Foyer.
- No food or beverages are permitted inside the Auditorium.
- Workshop Hall W1 session requires pre-registration and is capped at 60 participants.
- The Day 2 Investor Roundtable in VIP Room V1 is invite-only.
- Main event dietary requests close on 25 August 2026.
- Gala Dinner dietary requests close earlier, on 20 August 2026.
- Kosher meals require at least 10 days' notice and should be requested separately.
- All delegates must vacate the venue by 17:00 on Wednesday, 16 September.

## Accessibility

- Step-free access is available across all main event spaces.
- Hearing loop coverage is available in the Auditorium and Room A1 only.
- Delegates needing portable hearing support for Rooms B2, C1, or Workshop Hall W1 should request this in advance.
- Dedicated accessible seating is available in the Auditorium and breakout rooms.
- Accessible parking is available in P2 and passes are issued on registration.
- Guide dog relief area: Main Lobby North exit.
- If the Welcome Reception takes place on the Rooftop Terrace, lift access is available but capacity is limited. Attendees needing lift priority should notify the event team in advance.
